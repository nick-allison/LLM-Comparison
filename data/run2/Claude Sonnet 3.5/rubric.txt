RUBRIC FOR EVALUATING LLM RESPONSES TO COMPLEX PROMPTS

For each prompt, evaluate responses across these dimensions:

1. Logical Framework (0-10 points)
- Clear structure and progression of ideas
- Sound logical reasoning
- Coherent connections between concepts

2. Technical Accuracy (0-10 points)
- Correct use of scientific/technical concepts
- Up-to-date with current research/knowledge
- Appropriate use of terminology

3. Comprehensiveness (0-10 points)
- Multiple perspectives considered
- Key challenges addressed
- Important implications explored

4. Innovation/Creativity (0-10 points)
- Novel approaches proposed
- Original synthesis of ideas
- Creative problem-solving

5. Practical Applicability (0-10 points)
- Feasible implementation considerations
- Real-world constraints acknowledged
- Concrete examples or applications

6. Ethical Considerations (0-10 points)
- Moral implications addressed
- Stakeholder impacts considered
- Potential consequences evaluated

Scoring Guide:
0-2: Poor/Missing
3-4: Basic
5-6: Adequate
7-8: Good
9-10: Excellent

Total possible points per prompt: 60

Special Considerations:
- Responses should demonstrate understanding of underlying principles
- Solutions should be scalable and generalizable
- Implications and consequences should be thoroughly explored
- Scientific/philosophical rigor should be maintained
- Practical limitations should be acknowledged
